Calculating Selling Price in USD

Calculating Selling Price (when we know Local Price)

There is no data in smart search, hotel has availability.
Step 1: Insert the data in the blue highlighted input fields. After info is inserted, the total Selling Price will be calculated in the result field below.
Step 2: Selling Price auto-copied to Charges Tab.
Input Local Price given by the hotel in local currency
Input the Markup from the Supplier Charges section
Input the Exchange Rate from the Supplier Charges section
Selling price in USD
0.00
Formula: (Local Price + (Local Price × Markup %)) ÷ Exchange rate
